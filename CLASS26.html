About Me

I'm a passionate Junior Full Stack Developer with 6 months of hands-on internship experience in building scalable, user-friendly web and mobile applications. I'm driven by a deep interest in creating clean, efficient, and modern solutions that bring ideas to life through technology. My learning journey has been focused on mastering both frontend and backend development while writing code that is organized, maintainable, and visually appealing.

During my internship, I worked closely with experienced developers and participated in real-world projects involving responsive UI design, RESTful API integration, and database management. I'm eager to continue growing as a full-stack developer by contributing to meaningful projects that challenge my skills and creativity. My goal is to build applications that are not only functional but also optimized for performance, security, and scalability. I'm constantly learning new tools and frameworks to improve my efficiency and stay aligned with modern software development practices.
